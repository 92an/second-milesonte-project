Quizzmathica

This is a math game aimed at second and third graders. Here you get to practice the fundamental mathematic operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. This is a supplementary to class and not a substitute. Make sure you listen to you teachers!

The game has 4 modes, one for each basic operation. Click to select the one you want to practice. The game will then provide 10 questions on that topic. Do the calculation and fill it in the input box. Then click submit. After finishing all questions, you get sent back here to pick another game mode.
